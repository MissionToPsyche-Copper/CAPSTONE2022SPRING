Welcome to the Psyche Exhibit! Most of the Exhibit revolves around the asteriod Psyche. However, to look towards the future, we must first start with the Past. Namely, the origin of the name. Click on the three images below to find out more:

Unsurprisingly, the ancient greek panethon of gods had a goddess of Soul, and her name was Psyche.
Psyche often referred to the 'soul' of the individual, to the ancient greeks; the soul was often depecited as a butterfly.
Much like the butterfly, the Goddess of Soul will carry our spirit of exploration through a butterfly-like vessel, so that we may learn more about the asteriod, Psyche, and the universe's birth.
